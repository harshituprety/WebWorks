The Phantom Tollbooth by Norton Juster

In this childrens book we are introduced to Milo a young bored boy who recieves a package of the mysterious Toolboth. He puts this together and enters the booth to a new world to discover the wonder of numbers, words, and friends wth some problem solving and sage advice thrown in the mix.

This is one of the first books I rembember getting out of the primary school library and I found it a truly enjoyable rolicking ride into the world of fantasy. Here everyday things and ideas are turned upside down and inside out. If you havent read it, then you should. It doesnt take too long.
